# Cam Howey

**Brief Bio:** Cam Howey is a retired engineer with a passion for applying big data to healthcare, generosity, gratitude, and kindness. He lives in Calgary, Alberta. His current focus of work has been analyzing data from the [Canadian General Social Survey](https://www150.statcan.gc.ca/n1/en/catalogue/89F0115X) on giving, volunteering, and donation rates. He is an Excel magician and playful grandfather. He has been a [presenter at Mindcamp Canada.](https://www.mindcamp.org/presenters/past-presenters/?leader=howey_cam)
